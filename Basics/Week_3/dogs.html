Dogs (Canis lupus familiaris) are domesticated descendants of wolves and are widely considered "man's best friend" due to their loyalty and historical partnership with humans. They were the first animals ever domesticated, with evidence suggesting the process began over 25,000 years ago. Today, they are the most popular pet globally, found in approximately one-third of all households.
